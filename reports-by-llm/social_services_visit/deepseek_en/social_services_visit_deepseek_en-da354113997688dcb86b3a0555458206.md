

**Social Services Visit Report**

**Date:** October 02, 2024  
**Location:** Nakkila, Finland  
**Visit Type:** Family Reunification Progress Check  

---

### **Summary of Visit Purpose**

The purpose of this visit was to assess the progress toward family reunification for a multi-generational household in Nakkila. Given the moderate case severity and the household's financial difficulties, the visit aimed to evaluate the family's current situation, identify any challenges impacting their stability, and ensure ongoing support is provided.

---

### **Observations During the Visit**

During the visit, interactions within the multi-generational household revealed a mix of dynamics. The older generation expressed concerns about reduced financial resources affecting their quality of life, while younger members showed signs of stress from increased responsibilities. Notable observations included:

- **Financial Stress:** Family members reported difficulty managing basic expenses due to limited income and savings.
- **Emotional State:** Some individuals exhibited signs of anxiety related to financial instability and uncertain future plans.
- **Inter generational Tensions:** There were occasional conflicts arising from differing views on resource distribution, though overall communication remained cordial.

---

### **Assessment**

The household faces moderate risks primarily due to financial strain. The primary caregiver's reduced income has impacted family cohesion and mental health. No critical issues were observed, but ongoing support is essential to mitigate potential stress and maintain stability.

---

### **Recommended Actions or Follow-up**

1. **Mental Health Support:** Referral to a local social worker for mental health counseling to address financial-related stress.
2. **Financial Assistance:** Coordination with local authorities to explore additional financial resources or assistance programs.
3. **Follow-up Visit:** Scheduling a subsequent visit in two months to monitor progress and provide updated support.
4. **Referrals:** Consideration of community-based support groups for family dynamics facing similar challenges.

---

This report outlines the current situation, identifies key areas needing attention, and provides actionable steps to support the household's stability and well-being.
