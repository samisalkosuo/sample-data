While the project is progressing well, there are potential risks and blockers that require attention:

1. Scope creep: As we delve deeper into the requirements gathering phase, there is a risk of expanding the project's scope beyond its original objectives. To mitigate this risk, we have established clear guidelines for defining and prioritizing user requirements, ensuring that the project remains focused on its core objectives.
2. Vendor collaboration: Coordinating with external vendors for specific technical expertise can sometimes be challenging due to time zone differences and communication barriers. To address this issue, we have assigned a dedicated project manager to oversee vendor interactions and ensure smooth collaboration.
