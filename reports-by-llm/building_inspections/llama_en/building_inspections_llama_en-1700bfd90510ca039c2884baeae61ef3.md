Purpose of Inspection: Routine check for the municipality's property maintenance records.

**4. Observations and Findings**

- Structural:
  - The building's foundation appears solid, but some minor cracks were observed on the north wall.
  - The roof shows signs of aging, with some missing shingles and slight sagging in the center.
- Electrical:
  - All electrical connections seem to be secure, but some outlets show signs of wear and tear.
  - The lighting system is functional, but some fixtures are outdated.
- Plumbing:
  - Water pressure is satisfactory, but there were reports of occasional leaks under sinks.
  - Some pipes appear corroded, particularly in the older sections of the building.
- HVAC:
  - The heating system functions adequately, but it's noted that the temperature control might be less efficient than modern standards.
  - Air conditioning is not present or functional.
- Fire Safety:
  - Smoke detectors are installed and functioning correctly.
  - Exits are accessible and well-maintained.
- Accessibility:
  - Stairs have handrails, but they could be improved for better grip.
  - Doorways are wide enough for easy passage.

**5. Identified Issues and Recommendations**

1. Repair cracks in the foundation to prevent further damage.
2. Replace missing shingles on the roof to maintain its integrity.
3. Upgrade electrical connections to meet current safety standards.
4. Replace outdated lighting fixtures with energy-efficient alternatives.
5. Inspect and repair corroded pipes under sinks to avoid leaks.
6. Consider upgrading the heating system for better efficiency.
7. Install air conditioning units if not already present.
8. Improve handrails on stairs for better grip.
9. Ensure all doorways are wide enough for easy passage.

**6. Compliance Summary**

The building appears to be in compliance with local regulations, but some minor issues were identified that need attention. The municipality should consider addressing these concerns to maintain the property's integrity and ensure public safety.

**7. Conclusion**

This inspection highlights several areas of concern within the building. Addressing these issues will help maintain the property's value and ensure a safe living environment for its occupants. Further inspections are recommended every 5 years or as needed.
